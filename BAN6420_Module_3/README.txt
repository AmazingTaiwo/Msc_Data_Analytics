Explanation:
Classes:

Product: Represents a policy product. It includes methods to update the product, suspend, or remove it.
Payment: Manages the payments for policyholders. It includes methods to process a payment, send a reminder, and apply penalties for missed payments.
Policyholder: Represents a person with an insurance policy. It has methods to register, suspend, reactivate, add products, and display account details.
Demo Workflow:

Two products (Health Insurance and Car Insurance) are created.
Two policyholders (John Doe and Jane Smith) are registered.
Both policyholders are enrolled in the products, and payments are processed for the products they purchased.
Account details (including payments and products) are displayed for both policyholders.
Various actions such as suspending and reactivating policyholders and products are demonstrated.
